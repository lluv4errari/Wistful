Behind the Eye

They say a picture is worth 1000 words. What can these words be? Questions? Answers? Both? None? Are those words telling you a story behind the picture? Or rather, is it silent and letting you pick 1000 words?

In regards to photography, it truly is fascinating how concepts such as math and philosophy can tie in so deeply with photography, particularly in this art style. It makes you question and reflect on your own knowledge, despite the limited subjects presented to you in the photograph.

Intent

My eye for art stems from my fascination in photographs that evoke a feeling of uncertainty and possible surrealism within them. I hope for you to notice this in my work. I intend for these photographs to capture a sense of longing for nostalgia, but with a hint of eeriness underlying within, hence why I decided to call my online space “Wistful.”

Every photograph published on this site made me feel some uncertainty when experiencing the scene – both in real life while photographing, and looking at the photo itself. You may recognize my work as “liminal spaces,” which has been a great interest in the realm of art for me since 2018.

While I understand this particular style of photography may not be significant to everyone, but for those who do connect to it on a deeper level, such as myself, I would hope for you to see my vision and potential in these photographs which adhere to the topic of nostalgia and eeriness, thus making it unsettling and interesting nonetheless.
